
1. Business Objectives

The objective of the Influencer Marketing Analytics and Recommendation System is to help brands make informed influencer marketing decisions by providing tools for influencer discovery, engagement authenticity analysis, and campaign ROI evaluation. The system aims to reduce marketing risks, improve campaign effectiveness, and enable brands to maximize returns from influencer collaborations.

The platform will support organizations in selecting suitable influencers, identifying suspicious engagement patterns, and measuring the financial impact of influencer campaigns through affiliate link performance analysis.

Business Goals

BO-01: Improve Influencer Selection Process

Enable brands to discover and filter influencers based on category, subscriber count, views, and engagement metrics. This helps marketing teams identify influencers who best align with campaign objectives and target audiences.

BO-02: Detect Fake Engagement and Fraudulent Activity

Provide mechanisms to identify influencers with suspicious engagement patterns by analyzing likes, views, comments, and subscriber-to-engagement ratios. This helps brands avoid investing in influencers with artificially inflated metrics.

BO-03: Measure Campaign Return on Investment (ROI)

Allow brands to track affiliate-link-based conversions and revenue generated through influencer campaigns. The system will calculate ROI metrics to evaluate campaign effectiveness and support future investment decisions.

BO-04: Enhance Marketing Decision-Making

Provide analytical dashboards that present influencer performance, engagement statistics, campaign outcomes, and ROI metrics in a visual and easy-to-understand format.

BO-05: Reduce Manual Evaluation Efforts

Automate influencer filtering and performance assessment processes, reducing the time required for marketing teams to evaluate potential influencer partnerships.

Expected Business Benefits

- Improved influencer selection accuracy.
- Reduction in marketing spend on influencers with fake engagement.
- Better visibility into campaign performance and profitability.
- Faster evaluation and comparison of influencers.
- Increased effectiveness of influencer marketing campaigns.
- Improved utilization of marketing budgets through ROI tracking.

Success Criteria

- Accurate identification of suspicious engagement patterns.
- Successful recommendation of influencers based on campaign requirements.
- Ability to calculate and display campaign ROI using affiliate link data.
- Reduction in influencer evaluation time.
- Increased confidence in influencer selection decisions.

The proposed solution will provide brands with a centralized platform for influencer evaluation, campaign performance monitoring, and ROI measurement, enabling more effective and data-driven influencer marketing strategies.
