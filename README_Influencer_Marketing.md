# Influencer Marketing Analytics and Recommendation System

## Domain
Customer and Market Intelligence (CMI)

## Overview
The Influencer Marketing Analytics and Recommendation System helps brands discover suitable influencers, analyze audience compatibility, detect engagement fraud, track campaign performance, and make data-driven marketing decisions through interactive dashboards.

Source document: Influencer Marketing Theme Exploration Document.

---

## Introduction
Influencer Marketing is a digital marketing strategy where brands collaborate with social media influencers, content creators, and industry experts to promote products and services. The system aims to improve influencer selection, campaign effectiveness, and ROI measurement.

## Types of Influencers
- **Mega Influencers** – 1M+ followers
- **Macro Influencers** – 100K–1M followers
- **Micro Influencers** – 10K–100K followers
- **Nano Influencers** – 1K–10K followers
- **Brand Ambassadors** – Long-term brand advocates
